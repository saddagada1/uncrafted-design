our

process

01

brief

Send us a detailed brief with the following information: who you are, core values, business goals, target audience, what you sell, required features, scope of the project (or sitemap), timeframes and deadlines, examples of other websites you like and everything else you think is important.

02

discuss

We discuss approaches and concepts to find the perfect solution for your needs. Multiple sessions can be held if necessary. If we come to an agreement we proceed with the project.

03

design

This is where the magic happens. We will work on art direction, concept and design. In the first phase we present a mood board or 2-3 screens showing the look and feel of the final product. After the direction has been approved, we design the entire project.

04

develop

After approval of the design/mood, the respective screen design is developed. We will update regularly on the status of development so you always have a clear insight into the progress.

05

control

After development is complete, we continue to test on multiple browsers and devices to fix bugs. After your final approval, the site is ready to ship.
